# 🚀 LaunchLab

**Build. Survive. Scale.**

LaunchLab is an interactive **startup simulation game** where you play as a founder making real-world decisions — hiring a team, building product, finding market fit, and surviving on limited runway.

Unlike typical dashboards or simulators, LaunchLab is designed as a **decision-driven experience** inspired by games like *BitLife*, but focused on startups.

---

## 🎮 What You Do

* Start your own startup idea
* Make weekly decisions
* Manage cash, growth, product, and team
* Navigate challenges like churn, competition, and product issues
* Try to survive — or scale into a successful company

---

## 🧠 Core Gameplay

Every week, you face a situation:

> “Users are dropping due to poor UX. What do you do?”

You choose:

* Improve product
* Push marketing
* Ignore

Each decision has consequences.

---

## ⚙️ Features

### 📜 Timeline System

* Full history of your startup journey
* Weekly events, decisions, and outcomes
* Visual progression like a founder story

### 👥 Team Management

* Hire engineers, designers, marketers, etc.
* Balance growth vs burn
* Manage morale and efficiency

### 🧪 Product System

* Improve UX, fix bugs, add features
* Manage technical debt
* Build toward product-market fit

### 🌍 Market Strategy

* Choose target audience
* Set pricing and positioning
* Handle competition and demand

### 💀 Failure & Endings

* Run out of cash
* Lose all users
* Destroy your reputation

Or:

* Build a profitable business
* Scale fast
* Reach startup success

### 🏆 Achievements

* Unlock milestones (growth, revenue, survival, etc.)
* Track your founder journey
* Replay and improve

### 🎯 Game Mechanics

* Runway pressure (cash vs burn)
* Trade-off decisions (growth vs stability)
* Random events (market, product, team)
* Strategy-based outcomes

---

## 🎨 UI/UX

* Game-style interface (not a dashboard)
* Timeline-first experience
* Dark mode + Light mode
* Minimal glass UI (only for command module)
* Clean, solid, premium design

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* Frontend: Next.js / React
* Styling: Tailwind CSS
* State: Client-side state management
* Data: LocalStorage (for persistence)

---

## 📌 Project Goal

LaunchLab is not just a project — it’s an experiment to turn:

> **startup thinking into an interactive game**

The focus is on:

* decision-making
* trade-offs
* learning through simulation
* making complex concepts intuitive

---

## ⚠️ Current Limitations

* Fully client-side (no backend)
* Simplified economic and market models
* Not intended to be a perfect real-world simulation

---
